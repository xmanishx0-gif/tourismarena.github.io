The Taj Mahal is one of the most beautiful monuments in the world, located in Agra, India. It was built by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, and that is why it is also known as the symbol of love. Made completely of white marble, the Taj Mahal shines differently at sunrise, sunset, and even in moonlight.Inside the Taj Mahal, the tombs of Shah Jahan and Mumtaz Mahal are placed with stunning decorative work around them. The monument is surrounded by beautiful gardens, fountains, and symmetrical pathways that make it more attractive.
